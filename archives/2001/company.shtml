Welcome to GRIN - the world of High End Gaming.

GRIN was formed 1997 and is an incorporated firm with one of Sweden's largest IT venture capital companies (TBS) as a major owner. GRIN employs 15 people. We make games that plays fast and look good - simple as that.

Our 3D engine itself is a robust multi purpose platform. It has the suiting name the Diesel Engine™ and is used for pulling some heavy titles. The Diesel Engine™ allows our artists to freely design and create high-end graphics for your entertainment. The Diesel Engine™ allows chrome, bump mapping, specula lighting, bones, dynamic surfaces, 3D positional sound and music. All to make sure that there are no limits for the creative minds when setting up a GRIN Game. The engine was built to take full advantage of the potential of Xbox and Nvidias' upcoming chipsets. We work close with Nvidia on the implementation of their new hardware to deliver the best gaming quality possible.
