19th Oct 2001 FAQ and skins

Ballistics FAQ is up

As you can see we've added a FAQ option on the main menu. Here you can read our answers on the most frequently asked questions.

The raw skins are here!

Upon request we give you the raw skins for the Pilots and the Speeders. The skins can be edited in any program handling .tga files. To add a skin just put your latest creation into the "Skins" folder under either "Pilots" or "Speeders". The Skins can be named anything but their file extension must be .tga or .dds. If you use a DDS plugin this will save you some memory in game and that in turn gives you a better frame rate. To see the skins of others you must exchange them over e-mail. Also remember to turn the Force Speeder Skins option OFF and you will see all Speeders and Pilots in their individual skins. If you think you made a REALLY cool skin do send it over to us at HQ@Grin.se
Get into gear - raw skins right here!
