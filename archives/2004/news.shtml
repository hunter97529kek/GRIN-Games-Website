 3rd Nov 2001 

Ballistics Demo up!

The long awaited stuff is here! Now everyone can go Ballistics and throw themselves into the Speed trials. The demo offers three levels of geometry of the SkyTown track on Rookie, Pro and Ballistics level. There is also a quick tutorial for you.

The minimum specs are:

  • CPU 400
  • RAM 128
  • 3D-card: Geforce1/Voodoo4 or ATI Radeon
Also MAKE SURE to use the latest drivers from your 3D card vendor and if you have a VIA based motherboard please use the 4-1 driver available from the VIA.
This demo is not guaranteed under Windows XP using SoundBlaster Live and Audigy cards yet.
The demo is free to mirror and spread anyway and anywhere.

Download the demo right here and go ballistic!

- GRIN

- Welcome to the online Preimiere of GRIN!

GRIN is now ready to reveal its game and technology to the gaming community. We have stayed very quiet and in the dark for long. Our first appearance was the 3rd of September at ECTS 2000 stand NG 270. The purpose of this secrecy is of course to be able to develop our game and technology without giving away any half true promises to the gamers about what we will create and deliver. We now feel very comfortable with our setup; Ballistics™ the racing game of the future and the Diesel Engine™ the 3D technology behind the game. The visitors at ECTS were given the opportunity to check out Ballistics™ in action as well as play the thing. We’d like to congratulate Kevin Petersen from England who was the first guy on site to try Ballistics™ - going a full 1014 km/h down the city track.
