CDV announces the official Vultures website

Vultures: Website online!

CDV hits with Vultures new terrain, because this game will be an exclusive Xbox title, and the screens are already looking very promising. The game features motion capture animation and real-time shadows which will make your Xbox melt!
The official homepage has a nice set of screens and also a developer diary from our Swedish developer GRIN.
The screens already convey a nice, gothic mood, which is especially unique for the beat 'em up genre!
Loads of arenas filled with traps and in complete 3D featuring the latest technology await you, so don’t be square and visit the official homepage!

We have started sharpening our swords and smell the blood. Get yourself seated  upon the spectator stand cause some serious limb chopping is up ahead!
