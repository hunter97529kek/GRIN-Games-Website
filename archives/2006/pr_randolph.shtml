GRIN in LA

GRIN expands to the US

As of this month GRIN has established a US presence. Located in Los Angeles California, the operations are headed by Randolph Beverly Vice President of North American Operations. Having worked at industry forefront companies like EA, FOX and Disney Mr Beverly adds an important network and know how to GRIN.
Mr Beverly will be GRIN's point of contact for US publishers with a strong focus on the next generation consoles and heavy IP's.
"I am very pleased to be working with an organization as talented as GRIN, Inc. and I embrace the familiar by-product of our collective successes; the challenging vicissitudes of both the North American and global gaming communities that lie ahead shall remain within the scope of our crosshairs. On behalf of the organization and its leadership, our primary goal will be to focus on what we do best, which is to create fun and exciting new products for next generation consoles, and bring these products to gamers - wherever the opportunities may exist" said Randolph Beverly VP GRIN Inc. "I'm extremely satisfied with our choice of Mr Beverly. It will be a strength added to an already strong organization. Having a fast and no hassle contact directly with our US partners will make GRIN an even more accessible developer to work with. As we are now placing our next Xbox and Ps2 products for the 18 months production cycle to come this is perfect timing" said Bo Andersson, CEO GRIN Inc.

/GRIN


About GRIN

GRIN Inc, Stockholm Sweden, is a game developer bringing forth games on the PC and Xbox.
GRIN has been working closely with nVIDIA Inc. to utilize their latest GeForce series of cards in all of GRIN's present and upcoming games.
Specializing in pixel and vertex-shader technology, GRIN raises the value of high-end hardware with high-end games.
