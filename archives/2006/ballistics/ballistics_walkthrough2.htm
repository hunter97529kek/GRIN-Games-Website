Daredevil and Beyond - SpeedOfSound's Guide to Mach 3

Walkthrough

Here is the graphical walkthrough complete with tips and comments.
Some of this information is meant for the more advanced speedfreaks. The speeds shown in the pics is in kph. Divide this number by 1.609 to get mph. My (SpeedOfSound's) personal track record on Skytown is 00:59:46.

As stated on the previous page, the whole sequence of screenshots are numbered and put in order together with its accompanying comment and laptime. Click any screenshot to get a popup blowup of it. The popup window has a navigation bar so that you can jump forward and backward in steps of one screenshot at a time through the pictorial sequence. Normally there is one screenshot for every object on the track. If there is more than one picture in the same row it means that all these pictures show the same object at different distances (e.g. pic 08 and 09).
